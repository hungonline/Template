Our story

Vibeji originated from the founder’s own experience as a millennial who loves travelling and experiencing life differently. Tri spent most of his time living city by city in 2018 and observed a few things.

Tri found out that there was lack of diversified entertainment activities when he’s living or travelling to in a city. Most of the time locals and expats would just go for a movie, attend weekend workshops or hanging out for a drink while in reality, they can opt for more interesting and/or indie activities hosted by other people. Tri also noticed most of his friends are capable of providing such activities, by sharing what they know from their hobby or profession, and earn money from that. Tri started hosting an activity himself and invited his friends to join; he realised there’s a market for this, slowly rising up. A truely millennial-oriented service, and an Airbnb for entertainment activities.
